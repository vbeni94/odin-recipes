Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico.

It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro.

Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  • Step1
    1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic
    2. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  • Step2
    1. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
